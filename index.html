Breakfast Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp rapeseed oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of seeded wholemeal bread wholemeal bread
  • 2 handfuls of rocket

Method

  1. heat a pan of water, carefully break in the eggs and leave to poach for 1-2 mins until the whites are firm but the yolks are still runny.
  2. Halve and stone the avocado, then scoop out the flesh and smash onto the bread. Add the eggs, grind over black pepper and add a handful of rocket to each portion.

Dinner Recipe

Dinner

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dried gnocchi
  • Small bunch of kale
  • 2 finely chopped shallots or half an onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 59ml of dry white wine
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme thyme
  • 1 can full fat coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Black pepper to taste

Method

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
  2. Add the gnocchi and cook per packet instructions. When you have 30 seconds left add in the kale and wait for the gnocchi to float to the top.
  3. Drain the gnocchi and kale into a colander, return them to the pot, and toss with a dash of olive oil to prevent them sticking
  4. Coat the bottom of a large pot with 2 tablespoons of olive and place it over a medium heat.
  5. When the oil is hot, add the shallots and cook for 5 minutes, until soft.
  6. Add the garlic and cook for another 30 seconds, until fragrant
  7. Stir in the flour. Cook for about 2 minutes, until the flour forms a smooth paste and coats the shallots
  8. Stir in the white wine and thyme, and bring the liquid to a simmer. Allow to simmer for around 4 minutes or until reduced by half.
  9. Stir in the coconut milk. Bring it to a boil, lower heat and allow it to simmer for around 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until smooth and thick.
  10. Stir in the lemon juice, zest, gnocchi and kale. Cook for about 30 seconds more, stirring to coat the gnocchi and kale with sauce.
  11. Remove from the heat and season with salt and pepper and dish up
